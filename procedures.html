Coordinating involves managing participant outreach, scheduling, ensuring coverage for scans, cognitive tests, blood draws, and handling all necessary supporting documentation.

Documenting the Outcomes after Participant Visits

First, upload each participant's eligibility confirmation (recruitment report form) to the University of Pittsburgh's shared folder, and update the HEAD Study Screening and Enrollment Log.

Reimbursement Forms and DocuSign
Following each visit, a reimbursement form must be submitted so our participants are compensated. We find it easiest to use the Adobe Scan phone application to email a pdf to oneself in order to create a document in DocuSign for Brian and the Lead Coordinator.

OnCore
Within 24 hours following consent, each participant MUST be linked in OnCore. Note: training is required to gain access.

Epic
Following a participant's MRI, they will need to be linked within EPIC. Note: training through learn@work is required for the application to appear under your Citrix Workspace.

REDCap
Each participant visit will need to be logged into REDCap. Note: you must be added to projects by a supervisor to avoid permission issues.

CNDA Archiving
Each scan needs to be prepared for processing through CNDA.

Participant CSV Files

HEAD Study Processor Duties

Processing involves handling and validating scan data, ensuring proper processing, and uploading data to LONI, the centralized online repository for all study sites' data.

Training
Processors must be proficient in FreeSurfer (FS) and PET Unified Pipeline (PUP), software used to process MRI and PET scans, respectively. Resulting regional brain volumes and SUVRs are not completely accurate, so it is important to check output and make edits where necessary. Contact Sarah Keefe to set up FS training and Shaney Flores to set up PUP training.

LONI Imaging Uploads
A LONI account is required to access and install the IDA-Uploader application on your computer. To upload images, scans must first be downloaded from CNDA. Once this is completed, updtae the HEAD Processing Tracking Sheet.
